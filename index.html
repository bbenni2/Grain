What Grain does

Everything. Automatically.

Six things that used to take hours. Now zero effort.

100% Local AI

All analysis runs on your Mac via Ollama. Your photos never leave your machine. No account. No cloud. No data collection. Ever.

Smart Auto-Culling

Every photo scored on sharpness, exposure, histogram quality, and composition. Top 20% marked as stars. Rejects flagged automatically.

Time Estimate

Before you start, Grain shows exactly how many new photos it found and how long it'll take. No surprises.

Mac Stays Awake

Grain keeps your Mac from sleeping during the pipeline — no more waking up to a half-finished job. Automatically disabled when done.

Smart Dedup

Already processed photos are recognized instantly. Run Grain again and it only processes what's new — never duplicates work.

HDR Bracketing

Bracket series are detected, merged into HDR, and labeled — all automatically. Get publication-ready HDRs without touching software.
