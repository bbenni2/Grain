How it works

Three steps.
Zero hassle.

Grain is designed to disappear. You shouldn't have to think about it.

  1. 01

    Plug in your SD card

    Grain detects your card automatically. Or point it to any folder on your Mac.

    Auto-detection
  2. 02

    Press Start

    Grain culls, analyses, and archives while your Mac stays awake — no babysitting needed.

  3. 03

What Grain does

Everything.
Automatically.

Six things that used to take hours. Now zero effort.

  • 100% Local AI — total privacy

    All analysis runs on your Mac via Ollama. Your photos never leave your machine. No account required. No cloud upload. No data collection. Ever. Not even metadata.

    Zero network calls during analysis

  • Smart Auto-Culling

    Every photo scored on sharpness, exposure, histogram quality, and composition. Top 20% marked as stars.

  • Time Estimate

    ~6 min

    Before you start, Grain shows how many new photos and how long it'll take.

  • Mac Stays Awake

    No more waking up to a half-finished job. Grain keeps your Mac from sleeping during the pipeline.

  • Smart Dedup

    Already-processed photos are recognised instantly. Run Grain twice — no duplicate work.

  • HDR Bracketing

    Bracket series detected, merged, and labelled — automatically. Publication-ready HDRs without touching software.
